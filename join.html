No current vacancies? We are always open to reviewing applications from exceptionally motivated, creative computational biologists with a solid background in functional genomics data, programming skills, and the drive to take control of their own research. Before you get in touch, please take a moment to think about what it is that attracts you to our group and what you would like to achieve by joining us. We appreciate informal requests and personal(ized) applications. Mass emails and off-the-shelf applications will be ignored.
